About our company

Welcome to the legacy of Loupe Group International LLC, Founded in 1978. where excellence meets innovation with a rich legacy spanning over 35 years.

What began as a humble family-owned business in his hometown, has evolved into a global powerhouse with our headquarters operating in USA. Spreading across over 10 countries including the

  • USA
  • Germany
  • Singapore
  • Dubai
  • Saudi Arabia
  • Qatar
  • Malaysia
  • Sri Lanka
  • Maldives
  • Bangladesh

With an annual turnover exceeding USD 30 billion and a robust net asset value. We have earned our reputation as the most admired real estate development company and construction material trading company in the Europe.

We are the prominent players in international commodity trading industry, offering a comprehensive range of products in building materials. Furthermore, our finance solutions and investment initiatives empower small businesses to thrive and grow. Join us in shaping the future and discover the endless possibilities that await with Loupe Group International. With our journey of growth, empowerment, and transformation. the future is bright, and the possibilities are endless. As we take pride in our ability to adapt and thrive in diverse industries. Our joint ventures with leading companies in the market enable us to leverage expertise and resources, delivering unparalleled value to our clients and partners.
